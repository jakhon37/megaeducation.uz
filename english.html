Briefly about our Language Center:

MEGA EDUCATION Language Center was established on June 19, 2018 and currently operates seven teacher training centers. So far, 500 students have studied Korean and English at our academy, more than 200 of them are studying at Korean universities abroad.

Jeju National University: 25 students
Shinhan University: 15 students
Gachon University: 30 students
Hannam University: 30 students
Kyungpook National University: 5 students
Yongin Songdam University: 8 students
Joongbu University: More than 80 students
Semyung University: 3 and others.

At our training center, the students are tought by our highly qualified teachers. All teachers provide very efficient intensive educational programs in a short period of time based on their lesson plans.
